# 6B (British Pound Futures) — Strategy Learnings

## Instrument Profile
- **Point value**: $62,500/point
- **Min tick**: 0.0001
- **Commission**: $0.05/contract/side
- **Data**: 2016-01 to 2026-02 (~10 years, 5m + 1m + 1s)
- **Liquidity**: London session (03:00-08:25 ET) is the primary session for 6B. NY session has overlap but lower native GBP/USD volume. Asia is too thin.
- **Volatility**: Low daily ranges relative to ATR. FVGs are frequent but produce smaller R-multiples than equity index futures.

## Strategies Tested

### Continuation (bullish FVG -> long, bearish FVG -> short)
- **Status**: NO-GO
- **Tested**: LDN session, both directions + long-only + short-only, ORB15 + ORB30, rr 2.0/3.0/4.0, stop 5/10/15%, ATR 14/50, 1s magnifier
- **Result**: 72 configs tested per direction. Only 5/108 total configs marginally profitable (PF 1.00-1.03). Best: ORB15 rr=4.0 stop=5% ATR50 both directions: +29R/10yr, PF 1.03, DD -43R, Calmar 0.67.
- **Conclusion**: No structural edge in any direction or param combination. Do not revisit.

### Inversion Longs (bearish FVG -> long)
- **Status**: NO-GO
- **Script**: `run_6b_ldn_inv_long_pipeline.py`
- **Structural exploration**: 324 configs (3 dir x 2 ORB x 3 rr x 3 stop x 3 tp1 x 2 atr). Longs were clearly the profitable direction (Calmar 4.07 vs shorts 2.37). Shorts are weaker — do not test separately.
- **Anchor**: long-only, ORB30, rr=4.0, stop=15%, tp1=0.50, ATR 50, 1s magnifier
- **Phase 1 (Structural)**: PASS — 828 trades, 41.1% WR, +87.4R, PF 1.20, Calmar 4.07, DD -21.5R. But 4 negative years (2017, 2021, 2025, 2026) flagged inconsistency.
- **Phase 2 (Walk-Forward)**: PASS — 5 folds, WF eff 0.49, stability 0.80. However 2020-2021 OOS deeply negative (-5.6R, -11.1R). Combined OOS: 364 trades, +29.1R, PF 1.15.
- **Phase 3 (Prop Constraints)**: FAIL — avg annual R = 5.8 vs 18.0 threshold. Two negative OOS years killed the average.
- **Phase 4 (Hold-Out 2025-2026)**: FAIL — 84 trades, -16.8R, WR 29.8%, Sharpe -2.55, PF 0.69. Edge completely absent in recent data.
- **Phase 5 (Monte Carlo)**: FAIL — 59.8% survival at 25R ruin (threshold 60%). p50 DD = -23.2R.
- **Conclusion**: Structural Calmar inflated by 2023-2024 cluster. Strategy lacks regime consistency — 4/10 years negative, 2-year drawdown holes, and edge absent in 2025. Do not revisit without fundamentally different approach (gates, regime filter, etc.).

## Key Findings

- **Wide stops required**: stop=5% is catastrophic for 6B, stop=10% mostly negative. Only stop>=15% produces any edge. This is consistent with 6B's low volatility / tight ranges.
- **ORB30 > ORB15**: 30-minute ORB window consistently outperforms 15-minute across all strategies.
- **No robust edge found**: Continuation fails the robust pipeline. Inversion results INVALIDATED — tested without liquidity sweep gate, needs re-testing with sweep-gated definition. 6B's low volatility produces small R-multiples that don't survive transaction costs and regime changes.
- **Regime clustering**: Profitable years (2019, 2023-2024) cluster, suggesting the edge is regime-dependent rather than structural. Without a regime filter, the strategy is untradeable.
