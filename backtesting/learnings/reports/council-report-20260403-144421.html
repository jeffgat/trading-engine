Where the Council Clashes

GC is the central fight. Everyone agrees GC has quality (DSR 0.652, genuine diversification). The disagreement is operational: is the Apex ban a hard blocker (Contrarian, Principles) or a one-time friction cost (Expansionist, Outsider)?

Chairman sides with the Outsider's framing: the ban is a platform constraint, not a strategy disqualification. But the Expansionist's urgency is miscalibrated — 5/5 peer reviewers flagged it as the unanimous blind spot.

Blind Spots the Council Caught
